KAMO'S KAJUN GRILL— A TASTE OF LOUISIANA IN EAST TEXAS

Locally-owned and family-operated, Kamo's Kajun Grill brings the bold flavors of Louisiana to Tyler, TX. Founded by the Mitchell family, we're on a mission to serve up the most mouth-watering Cajun-inspired burgers around.

SIGNATURE CAJUN BURGERS

Sink your teeth into our juicy burgers, piled high with fresh toppings on toasted buns. Don't miss our fan-favorite Boudin Burger, featuring spicy Louisiana-style sausage. Pair it with a side of our addictive seasoned fries.

CONVENIENT LOCATION

Find us at 16700 Old Jacksonville Hwy in Tyler. Enjoy your meal at our outdoor picnic tables, perfect for a laid-back family dinner. Open Tue-Sat 11:30am-2:30pm & 5pm-7pm. Walk-up and drive-thru available.

FROM OUR KITCHEN TO YOUR TABLE

At Kamo's, we believe that great food brings people together. That's why we put our heart and soul into every burger we make. Using time-honored family recipes, we craft each dish with care, so you can taste the love in every bite.

SMALL BUSINESS, BIG IMPACT

As a small, family-owned restaurant, we pour our passion into every aspect of Kamo's Kajun Grill. From the moment you walk up to our window or drive thru, you'll feel the warmth and care we put into our food and service.
